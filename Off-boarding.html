OFF-boarding 

Getting prepared for employment termination

Off-boarding employees

When exiting and employee, organizations should want leavers to become advocates of their business by speaking highly, potentially send future profiles desire working for the organization or even simply leave a Glassdoor review.

In order to achieve a successful off-boarding, employees need to be supported, heard and valued every step of the exit way.

Accepting the resignation

When managers receive the resignation of their direct reports, the first step is to have a chat with them to address why the employee is leaving, if there is anything that they could do to retain them. Not having such chat would appear to the employee as if the organization and manager do not value them and do not event try to make them change their mind. Only in some cases the employer can indeed retain the employee as in the majority of the cases, they have already accepted an offer elsewhere and the time allocated to the manager is too short to get an compensation increase or a new growth plan.

After this chat, the manager should straight away plan the handover to the other member of the team and split the responsibilities accordingly, awaiting for a replacement to be found.

Exit survey

Conducting an exit survey for leaving employees can not only provide precious information as the employees will feel more free to write honest answers but will also give them a sense of entitlement to improve the business for current and future employees, which is the case if the business addresses these areas of improvements mentioned.
