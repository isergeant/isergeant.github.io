REDUNDANCY

 Redundancy is dismissal from a job, caused by the employer needing to reduce the workforce. 

Main reasons for redundancy
Operational changes
The arrival of new technology
Changes in the market
In order to reduce business costs

Principal actors
 Employee at risk of redundancy
HR Manager
If relevant, representative chosen by the employee
Tips 
 No surprises
keep the employee well informed throughout the process
Document your every step
