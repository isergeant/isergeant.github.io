Ensure Compliance with the Employment (Allocation of Tips) Act 2023 with Ascolta HR



What’s New?


Starting 1 July 2024, the Employment (Allocation of Tips) Act 2023 mandates that all tips must be passed on to employees without deductions. Non-compliance could result in fines up to £5,000 per employee.


Key Changes


- Fair Tip Distribution: Tips paid by cash or card must be allocated fairly to all employees, including those on zero-hour contracts.

- No Deductions: Employers cannot deduct bank or admin charges from tips.

- Timely Payments: Tips must be paid to employees by the end of the month following the receipt.


Why This Matters


Businesses in tipping industries must:

- Develop a Written Policy: Clearly outline how tips are allocated and distributed.

- Maintain Records: Keep accurate records of tip distribution. Employees can request this information every three months.

- Act Now: With no transition period, immediate compliance is necessary to avoid penalties.
