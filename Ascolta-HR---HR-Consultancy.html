Who we are

Ascolta HR is a comprehensive Human Resource Consultancy Service dedicated to supporting small businesses by providing access to their own HR Manager on demand. We also act as advisors for employees who find themselves in need of HR assistance, offering guidance and support through challenging situations. Our services include providing customisable templates and policies to ensure your business is HR compliant, along with offering retained packages that feature both Virtual and on-site HR options. Additionally, we handle ad-hoc project work, tailored to meet the unique needs of your organisation.
