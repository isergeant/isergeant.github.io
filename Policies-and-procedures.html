POLICIES & PROCEDURES

T​hese documents consolidate in the Employee Handbook or Company Handbook is a crucial piece to manage employee's expectation and create bespoke guidelines.

Examples of policies are:

  • recruitment and selection
  • code of conduct
  • leave and time off
  • performance management, grievance and disciplinary
  • Health and safety
  • Equal employment opportunity 
  • Training and development

This list is not exhaustive and there is a plethora of topics that can be tackled in the handbook. It's also the perfect opportunity to display the company values.
